Hello!

I am a mathematican and data analyst currently working on my PhD (expected 2026) under the direction of Isabel Darcy and William Thiel at the University of Iowa.

My primary research is in topological data analysis, a field which touches algebraic and geometric topology, statistics, and computer science; it also frequently involves collaboration with experts from non-mathematical fields, such as biology or neuroscience.
